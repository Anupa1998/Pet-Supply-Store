about us

Welcome to Paws & Whiskers! We are a passionate team dedicated to providing high-quality pet products for your furry friends. Whether you're looking for nutritious food, fun toys, cozy bedding, or grooming supplies, we’ve got everything your pet needs. We believe pets are family, and we strive to offer products that will make their lives healthier, happier, and more comfortable.

At Paws & Whiskers, we only stock trusted brands and products that meet the highest standards. Our goal is to be your one-stop shop for all things pet-related, offering great customer service and a seamless shopping experience every time.

our service

Pet Grooming


Treat your pet to a pampering session at our grooming salon. Our experienced groomers provide personalized grooming services tailored to your pet's breed, size, and individual needs, leaving them looking and feeling their best.

Veterinary Services


Keep your pet in tiptop shape with our comprehensive veterinary services. From routine check-ups and vaccinations to urgent care and preventive treatments, our skilled veterinarians are here to ensure your pet's well-being every step of the way.

Pet Training


Unlock your pet's full potential with our professional training programs. Whether you're looking to teach basic obedience commands or address specific behavioral issues, our certified trainers will work with you and your pet to achieve lasting results.
